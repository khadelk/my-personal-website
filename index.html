Hi there, I'm Sarah!

I was born and raised in Cedar Rapids, Iowa and moved to Iowa City in 2016 to attend the University of Iowa. I study Informatics with a focus on Human Computer Interaction. I'm interested in using technology and the internet to better peoples lives, bring us together and solve pressing issues.

My interests range over a whole variety of areas--including activism, art, tech and music.

Over the years, I've worked to elect Deidre DeJear, advocated for undocumented workers at the Center for Worker Justice, spent 6 months in Las Vegas putting on the presidential caucus and digitally organized a progressive US senate campaign in the midst of a pandemic. Those experiences made me realize how integral tech is to making organizing and activism accessible, transparent, and efficient. I'd like to spend my time delving further into that. At Iowa I'm involved with the Arab Students Association and WICs (Women in Computing Sciences).

In my free time I like to sing, play guitar and knit. I can also make a mean batch of chocolate chip cookies. My favorite color is forest green and my favorite flavor of ice cream is pistachio.
